Enter Your Amazon Device Code

Visit Amazon.com/code to enter the activation code displayed on your TV screen and register your Amazon device. This guide helps you complete the process quickly without confusion.

ENTER CODE & ACTIVATE →

When you open Prime Video or an Amazon-supported app on your smart TV or streaming device, a unique code appears on the screen. This code must be entered at amazon.com/code to link your device with your Amazon account.

Once the code is verified, your device is registered instantly and you can start streaming movies, TV shows, and Amazon Originals without interruption.

Why Amazon Asks You to Enter a Code

The device code system is designed to securely connect your TV or streaming device to your Amazon account without typing login credentials on the TV screen.

This method reduces login errors and improves account security during device activation.

How to Use Amazon.com/code to Activate Your Device

First, open the Amazon Prime Video app or Amazon-supported service on your TV. You will see a 5–6 digit activation code on the screen.

On your phone or computer, go to amazon.com/code, sign in to your Amazon account, and enter the displayed code exactly as shown.

Devices That Use Amazon.com/code

Amazon.com/code is commonly used for Fire TV, smart TVs, streaming sticks, game consoles, and other media devices that support Amazon Prime Video.

The activation steps remain similar across most devices.

What to Do If the Code Does Not Work

If the code is rejected, make sure it has not expired. Codes are time-sensitive and may need to be refreshed by reopening the app on your device.

Also confirm that you are logged into the correct Amazon account before entering the code.

Tips for Smooth Amazon Device Activation

Keep your TV and internet connection stable during activation. Avoid refreshing the browser while entering the code to prevent session errors.

Completing the process in one attempt ensures faster registration and uninterrupted streaming.
